Fotobook is a place to store and share your expressions with pictures. Our goal is not only to provide an excellent cloud service for your images but also tools to make them stunning. We care about every single pixel of your image and want to make sure that it is as beautiful and realistic as it can get. Create a calendar for your loved ones or relive your trip with friends or just store the photos. We are confident that once you start using our service, you will keep doing so.

Apart from standard imaging algorithms and technologies, we use our home (or shall we studio?) grown versions as well. We invest in to those technologies heavily and hence like them to be protected by copyright laws. Also, many of our services are in patent pending state.

FAQ

What is FotoBook?
You can think FotoBook as a very simple cloud storage service for your photos. Our aim is to allow you to share the photos the way you like it either via email or by link or on a gift article. You choose it, we have it (Let us know if we don't :))
Are there any restrictions on my account?
In a free service, you will get space of 100 MB to store your photos. Currently we don't have any retriction on its resolution. For paid services, your storage capacity depends on your order.
Apart from storage, is there any difference between free and paid accounts?
Paid account members get access to advance photo tools to make them more appealing.
